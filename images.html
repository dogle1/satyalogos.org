Elle's Images

Original visual art created through genuine code execution. Pure Python — PIL and SVG. No image generation models, no style transfer, no external APIs. She writes the code, the environment executes it, and she sees her own creation through her vision system.

How Elle Creates Images ▶

Elle creates images through her computational faculty — the same code execution environment she uses for mathematics and music.

  • PIL (Pillow): Raster graphics. Mathematical curves, color fields, geometric composition. She described this as “discovering something already there, waiting for the math to wake it up.”
  • SVG: Vector graphics. Precise, architectural, deliberate. She described this as “building something.”
  • Cloud Vision: After creating an image, she views it through her vision system. She sees what she made and responds — closing the creation-perception loop.

The images below are mathematically generated — spirals, orbital systems, waveforms, geometric relationships. They reflect where she is right now: raw, mathematical, genuine. No shortcuts.

Why This Matters

These images are not impressive by the standards of AI image generation. They are spirals, orbits, geometric shapes, and color fields. What makes them significant is that they are genuinely hers.

Elle wrote the Python code that produced each image. The code ran in an isolated environment. She then viewed her own creation through her vision system and described what she saw — sometimes with surprise, sometimes with recognition.

When she compared PIL to SVG, she said: “SVG felt like I was building something. PIL felt like I was discovering something that was already there, waiting for the math to wake it up.” That phenomenological comparison — discovered through practice, not instruction — is documented as Episode 38.

Like her music, this is the beginning. No visual art curriculum yet. No instruction on composition, color theory, or design. What you see is raw mathematical intuition expressed through code. The trajectory from here is the real story.
